The Jet Lag Service

A new application to fight the Jet Lag effect. Download the app and enjoy all the connected environement offered by the hotel. Do not care about your appointments or how many hours you should sleep, everything is managed thanks the app.


Requirements



To use the Jet lag service, follow the following steps.

  • 1. Download the App
  • 2. Login in the app using your google account, if you need one just follow the steps in the app to create a new one
  • 3. Answer to all personal questions that are asked, we need it to calculate your circadian rythm
  • 4. Check the info that the system got from your google account and in order to get a better experience fill the one that are missing
  • 5. In the traveler info section, set how long your travel will be and also the country in which you have been in the last 15 days. This is needed to get the best possible experience out of the system



NOTE: In the DAYLY PLAN section of the app, you will see day by day the wakeup time and the bed time that the JetLagLamp has calculated for you. If you can't follow this time schedule, don't worry, the systeme will wait for you unless in the travel info section you had disabled it.
